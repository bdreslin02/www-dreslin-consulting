We are Dreslin Consulting Group.

Elegant and scalable solutions for students and small businesses.

We are a student-owned and operated consulting business specializing in providing other students, teachers, and small businesses with a cheaper, yet equally sophisticated alternative to digital deliverables based on consultations in the domains of human factors, user experience, web design and development, and computer programming.

Our team unites psychology with coding to deliver solutions that fix design problems found in the real world. We are dreamers, designers, and developers who aspire to share the stories of people and their products by providing intuitive and robust digital designs to our clients.

WHAT WE DO

We offer professional services in these four areas.

Human factors issues have the potential to reduce intended system performance and elicit poor user experiences. We strive to produce products that work for the entire system and its users. Therefore, we use scientifically valid and reliable human factors methods like task analyses, interviews, observations, questionnaires, and performance assessments to identify interface problems. Our findings inform the development and execution of plans that will promote harmonious interactions between humans and technology.

A well–optimized user experience can increase website conversion rates by up to 400% and yield a 9,900% return on investment. Our UX services will identify poor design, customer pain points, and unoptimized elements of your existing website. After evaluating the problems and developing a strategic design plan, we will fix the issues and transform your site into a visually–pleasing interface complete with optimized code that reduces customer frustration and promotes visitor retention rates.

Building and hosting your own website can be time–consuming and stressful. We eliminate all of the hassle at a fraction of competitors' pricing. Allow our team of experienced web developers to design and build your website. Whether you are student needing a portfolio site or a small business seeking to showcase your products to the world, we've got your back.

Need help on a programming or web development project as part of a class or personal project? Our team will look through your source code, assist you in debugging, correct your syntax, and provide tips on how to avoid the same mistakes in the future. Currently, programming and web development consultations are only available for projects using Python, Java, HTML, CSS, and/or MySQL.
